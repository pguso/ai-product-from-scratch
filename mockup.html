Alternative Approaches
Option A
"Do you think you'll be able to send the document today?"
Impact: Removes accusatory tone by framing as a question about capability rather than blame. Maintains the deadline while inviting honest assessment of feasibility.
Collaborative Respectful Clear deadline
Option B
"I need the document today to move forward — let me know if that's an issue."
Impact: Provides context about why timing matters and acknowledges potential obstacles. Creates space for dialogue rather than forcing compliance.
Transparent Context-rich Direct
Option C
"Just checking in about the document. What's the current status?"
Impact: Completely removes pressure and blame. Most effective when maintaining relationship quality is more important than immediate speed.
